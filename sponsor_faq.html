Conference Sponsor Logistics

What do I need to bring?
If your sponsorship includes an onsite presence for your team such as a lounge or exhibit table, we will connect you with our vendor who will assist you in securing and setting up your onsite presence. This includes renting furniture as well as set up & break down of lounges or exhibits.
How to get stuff to the venue?
If your sponsorship includes an onsite presence for your team we will connect you with our vendor who will assist you in securing and setting up your onsite presence. This includes renting furniture, set up/break down of lounges or exhibits.
Can I ship stuff to the venue?
Shipping to the venue is limited to package arrivals May 2nd & 3rd. Please note: You’ll be responsible for carrying out all undistributed materials at the end of all events. Ship to:
[Sponsor Company Name] URx Conference
The Hilton Parc 55 Hotel
55 Cyril Magnin St.
San Francisco, CA 94103
Can I bring my own signage and props?
Yes, with advance approval, we allow external signage, equipment, and props in the venues.

How sponsorship works

How can I sponsor or exhibit at a URx event?
Please complete our sponsorship form, and include the event you’re interested in sponsoring and whether you’re interested in a specific opportunity. We’d be happy to help you find the opportunity that best fits your goals.
What will you need after I’ve committed to a sponsorship opportunity?
Once you’ve signed and returned our sponsor agreement, we’ll ask for: a high-resolution (preferably vector) logo in both full color and single color, a URL we can use for our site and event materials, and a 200-character description of your company. If you’ve selected a lounge, interactive hangout, or swag bag add-on, we’ll set up a kickoff to discuss the execution of your sponsorship.
Do you offer discounts if I commit to more than one event this year?
We’d love to see you at multiple events this year! Supporting multiple events shows a deep commitment to the URx Community and we would certainly recognize that. Any discount is based on the level of sponsorship and opportunities selected.
I am interested in multiple events, but I can only commit to one right now. Will you still honor the multi-event discount?
We can apply the multi-event discount at the time your second event is booked. Unfortunately, we can’t extend a retroactive discount to past events.
The sponsorship I want is listed as “sold out”. Is there anything I can do?
We can always add you to a waitlist and reach out if a similar opportunity becomes available. We’d also be happy to discuss custom options that meet your needs.

What’s included

What is included in my sponsorship?
Each sponsorship comes with a different set of benefits and perks. All sponsorships include: a logo with linked URL on the event website, logo placement in communications to attendees before and after the event, logo placement on signage at the event, and complimentary tickets (quantities based on sponsorship level).
Do I get to speak at the event if I sponsor?
Speaking opportunities are not guaranteed with sponsorship, but we highly encourage sponsors to submit a speaker proposal when submissions are open.
We also have dedicated blocks in our event program to highlight sponsors at certain sponsor levels.
Will I be able to demo my product at the event?
Certain sponsorships include lounges and exclusive space for you to use to demo your product or take private meetings.
Can you offer additional tickets to the conference beyond what comes with my sponsorship?
Beyond the complimentary tickets included with your sponsorship, we offer a discount on additional tickets. Just let us know if you’d like to request additional tickets.
Do I need to handle the production of anything for my sponsorship?
Unless otherwise noted, we take care of everything related to the production of your event presence. If you’ve selected an art installation, interactive hangout, or swag bag add-on, we’d love for you to be involved in the creative process for your design, and we’ll take care of the rest.

How lead generation works

How can I capture leads at the event?
To make sure we never sacrifice lead quality for quantity, we don’t provide lead scanners at our events. However, there are still plenty of ways to gather attendee information—and we’re happy to help you set those up. If you ask former sponsors of our events, they’d assure you that you’ll still collect a robust list of highly-quality leads, and that our attendees are more eager to connect outside of URx Events because of the focus on conversation instead of scanning.
Are there additional ways to engage with attendees outside of the event?
Sponsors at the Delight and Engage levels are encouraged to organize social events for attendees when our event activities aren’t scheduled, and we’d be happy to include information about your gatherings in our communication with attendees.
I’d like to level up my presence the conference this year. What would you recommend?
This year we’re offering a variety of add-on opportunities for our sponsors to increase your exposure and engagement at the event. From logo placement to creative swag in event totes, we’ve got lots of ways for you to maximize your presence at the event. Learn more about add-on opportunities.
