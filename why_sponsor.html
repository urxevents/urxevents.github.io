Why sponsor a URx event

With a full focus on building the best experience possible, our events give you a new way to showcase your brand and engage with our community. We help you find the sponsorship opportunity that’s right for you, and you make connections as it comes to life.

More than events, more than a conference

We stand by values like these to create experiences that continously raise the bar foro our events—and keep our community coming back for more.


Engage > sell

Our community is a huge part of our programs and events from speaker & organizers to attendees and volunteers. We build engaging relationships, not transactional connections.

Passion > consensus

Our events aim to amplify the ideas that challenge us from a wide range of people and industries, not just the most popular ones.

People > technology

Our events tell the stories of the individuals behind the tools, not just the tools themselves.

Who you’ll reach

While we primarily design experiences for university recruiters, people from every part of the recruiting industry come to our events to learn best practices, share ideas, and explore new recruiting products and technologies. From recruiters, program managers to talent leaders, attendees care about collaboration, data, and strategies. The tools that make their teams just a little more efficient.
