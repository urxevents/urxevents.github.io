URx Conference is an annual two-day event created for and organized by Early Talent Recruiters & Developers.

URx Conference brings together the community of passionate early talent recruiting & development professionals. URx community members help companies recruit, develop, and retain great talent.

Our annual conference is a platform for engaging, thought-provoking conversations and discussions. URx Conference attendees nurture existing partnerships, grow networks and build community.
