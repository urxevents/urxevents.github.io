URx Conference

May 2020

San Francisco, CA

URx Conference is an annual two-day event created for and organized by Recruiters & Developers of early career talent.

Conference Website

URx Conference brings together the community of passionate early talent recruiting & development professionals. URx community members help companies recruit, develop, and retain great talent.

Our annual conference is a platform for engaging, thought-provoking conversations and discussions. URx Conference attendees nurture existing partnerships, grow networks and build community.

300 Attendees

15 Sponsors

2 Days + Workshops

We care a lot about having quality conversations and staying on top of what is happening in the Early Career Talent Aquisition space. Our portfolio companies that attend and the recruiting & development professionals we meet as sponsors are genuinely interested in a two-way conversation and sharing ideads with our companies, so we end up getting so much more out of URx events than your typical conference.

Luke Beseda

VP of Talent Infrastructure, Lightspeed Venture Partners

Engage

Maximize engagement with a prominent presence and full integration into our community experience.

Options Included at this level

Engage sponsorships are for companies that seek a high level of personal interaction with attendees and speakers. Featuring the largest attendee lounge and the opportunity to host a conference event of your choice. These opportunities are the perfect way to connect with attendees in a meaningful way.

Sponsors at the Engage level have a unique opportunity to collect attendee emails prior to the event, based on opt-in responses to the final pre-conference email.
