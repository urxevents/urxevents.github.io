About

UR(x) is is an annual conference bringing together university job developers, tech talent leaders, and student leaders. Conference attendees, partners and sponsors all have a vested interest in creating, maintaining, or advancing great university recruiting or employer relations program. Join us as we share best practices, discuss challenges, and explore solutions all while establishing great relationships.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the conference about?

UR(x) is is an annual conference bringing together university job developers, tech talent leaders, and student leaders. Conference attendees, partners and sponsors all have a vested interest in creating, maintaining, or advancing great university recruiting or employer relations program

I will be travelling to the conference, where should I stay?

There are a number of great hotel options in Palo Alto and the surrounding area with easy access to the Maguerrite, Stanfords Free Shuttle. In addition a number of attendees are arriving early to spend the weekend in San Francisco and will be commuting from the city.

How do I get to Stanford?

Stanford is convenienctly located equidistant from both San Francisco International Airport (SFO) and San Jose Interntional Airport (SJC). If you're driving, we will provide parking at the venue. Whether you are staying in Palo Alto or traveling from elsewhere in the San Francisco Bay Area, you can reach Stanford by way of convenient public transportation. Please visit this link for more info.

When should I arrive / depart?

The conference will begin on Monday evening with the sponors dinner. You should try to land in the early afternoon on Monday so you don't miss the dinner. The conference will end on Wednesday early evening by 5pm.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer full refunds for your ticket before October 15th. After that, we won't be able to provide refunds. You are always welcome to transfer your ticket to someone else if you let us know.

What meals are provided?

The Kickoff Dinner, breakfast, lunch, snacks, and all the coffee you can drink throughout the conference are included with your registration. Please let us knoww if if you have any special dietary requirements. We are happy to accomodate any dietary requests made in advance of the conference.

Who organizes UR(x)?

UR(x) is organized by a group university administrators and university recruiters with support from great partners and sponsors.
